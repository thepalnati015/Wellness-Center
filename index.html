FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS(FAQs)


What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

Call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. Provide clear details about the situation to get the fastest help.

How can I check symptoms online?

Use our Symptom Checker tool to enter your symptoms and get preliminary advice on possible conditions.

What is the best way to contact a healthcare provider?

Contact your healthcare provider directly via their phone number or through their online patient portal for non-emergency inquiries.

How do I find the nearest hospital?

You can use our Hospital Finder tool or search online maps for hospitals near you.

Where can I find health resources and articles?

Visit our Health Resources section for articles and guides on various health topics.
